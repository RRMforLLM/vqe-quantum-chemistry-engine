5 pasos de tony para hacer un pitch
1. orden de impacto ( lo más impactante de la solución va primero ) / hook ( arrebatar la atención ) / ¿mi abuela entiende? ( al juez no le importan los tecnicismos )
2. hazlo tuyo ( si el juez no entiende tu proyecto, nada de lo que hiciste le importa )
3. lenguaje corporal ( es tu turno, disfruta el momento )
4. cierre y preguntas ( la gente se va recordando el final ) / los jueces son personas que quieren comprender el proyecto
5. las slides ( minimalismo, queremos comunicar, no distraer )

Draft:
- Explain what YOUR problem is ( spending tons of time planning materials synthesis )
- Show the algorithm
- Recording of use ( along with a simultaneous explanation, talk on top )
- Mention numbers ( market )
- Mention validation ( numbers as well, surveys, interviews, etc. )
- Acknowledge and brief alternative solutions - competitors ( and why your solution is better )
- Mention yourself/why you should be chosen
- And make them want to reach out afterwards, want to know more
- Leverage future steps
- Hint to frame it as the investigation project it is
- Keep it in mortal terms
